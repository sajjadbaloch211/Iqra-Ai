### IQRA UNIVERSITY SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID 2025-2026

MERIT-BASED SCHOLARSHIPS:
- Coverage: 10% to 100% of tuition fee reduction.
- AT ADMISSION: Based on previous academic results (Inter/A-Levels) and IU Entrance Test score.
- CONTINUING STUDENTS (From 2nd Semester):
  - 4.0 GPA: ~60% scholarship on next semester tuition.
  - 3.75 - 3.99 GPA: ~40% scholarship.
  - 3.50 - 3.74 GPA: ~20% scholarship.
- REQUIREMENT: Minimum CGPA 3.5 is needed to be eligible for merit scholarship for continuing students.
- COURSE LOAD: Students must maintain a full course load (BS: 15+ cr, MS: 9-12 cr).

TALENT-BASED SCHOLARSHIPS:
- Eligibility: Excellence in sports, arts, or cultural activities.
- Requirement: Documented evidence of participation/achievement at National or Provincial levels.

NEED-BASED FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE:
- Target: Students facing genuine financial hardship.
- REQUIREMENT: Proof of income (salary slips, tax returns, death certificate of guardian if applicable).
- QARZ-E-HASANA: Interest-free loans are available for deserving students to cover education costs.

GENERAL SCHOLARSHIP RULES:
- One Scholarship: Students can only avail one scholarship at a time (whichever is higher).
- Discretion: University reserves the right to withdraw or reduce scholarships based on student behavior or performance.
