### IQRA UNIVERSITY ADDITIONAL INFORMATION & STUDENT SERVICES

1. STUDENT SOCIETIES & CLUBS:
- Iqra University Marketing Society (IUMS): Focuses on marketing events and industry exposed.
- IU Computing Society (IUCS): Organizes hackathons, coding competitions, and tech talks.
- IU Media Society: Handles film festivals, photography workshops, and media events.
- Sports Society: Manages inter-university sports competitions (Cricket, Football, Table Tennis).
- Social Welfare Society: Dedicated to community service and social causes.
- Literary & Debating Society: Enhances students' communication and rhetorical skills.

2. TRANSPORT & SHUTTLE SERVICES:
- Shuttle Routes: Iqra University provides shuttle services for students across various routes in Karachi (Main, North, Gulshan, Bahria Town).
- Standard Routes: Routes cover areas like Malir, Gulshan, North Nazimabad, Federal B Area, and Defense.
- Timing: Shuttles usually operate in three shifts: 
  - Morning (07:30 AM arrival)
  - Afternoon (01:15 PM departure)
  - Evening (04:15 PM and 05:45 PM departures)
- Points: Dedicated collection points are assigned for each route. Students must register for transport at the start of the semester.

3. LIBRARY FACILITIES:
- Digital Library: Access to HEC Digital Library, IEEE, and various international journals.
- Physical Library: Extensive collection of books on Management, Engineering, Health Sciences, and Fashion.
- Study Spaces: Quiet study zones and group discussion rooms available.
- Timing: Usually open from 08:30 AM to 09:00 PM on weekdays.

4. CAREER SERVICES & PLACEMENTS:
- Placement Office: Assists students in finding internships and jobs.
- Job Fairs: Annual career fairs are held where top companies (Engro, HBL, Meezan Bank, etc.) recruit IU graduates.
- Alumni Network: Strong network of 50,000+ alumni globally who help in mentoring and placements.
- Placement Rate: Iqra University boasts a 90%+ placement rate for its graduates within 6 months of completion.
