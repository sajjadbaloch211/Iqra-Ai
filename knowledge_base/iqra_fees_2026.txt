### IQRA UNIVERSITY FEE STRUCTURE (UPDATED FOR 2025-2026 ACADEMIC YEAR)

GENERAL FEE COMPONENTS (One-Time & Per Semester):
- Admission Fee (One Time): PKR 10,000 (Non-Refundable)
- Registration Fee: PKR 1,000
- Student Fund: PKR 1,250
- Library Fund: PKR 950
- Sports Fee: PKR 850
- Technology Fee: PKR 970
- Miscellaneous Fee: PKR 500
- Degree Fee & Convocation Fee: Charged separately at the time of graduation.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM FEES (BSCS & BBA):
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: PKR 6,200
- Standard Semester Load: ~18 Credit Hours
- Total First Semester Estimated Fee (Including General Fees): PKR 127,120
- Total Credit Hours for full program: 137 Credit Hours

GRADUATE & POSTGRADUATE FEES:
- MS Media Sciences (Film & TV Production): ~PKR 4,850 per credit hour.
- MS Programs (5th Semester onwards): PKR 10,000 semester registration fee.
- PhD Programs (7th Semester onwards): PKR 30,000 semester registration fee.

IMPORTANT FEE POLICIES:
- Annual Fee Increase: The university reserves the right to increase fees by up to 10% annually or before the start of each Fall semester.
- Scholarships: Academic excellence can earn students a 25% to 50% discount on tuition fees.
- Payment Methods: Online portal, designated bank branches, or installments (by contacting Finance Dept).
- Penalties: Late fee submission results in penalties as per university rules.
