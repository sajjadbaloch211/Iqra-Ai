### IQRA UNIVERSITY ACADEMIC POLICIES AND RULES (2025-2026)

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- Minimum Requirement: 75% attendance in each course is mandatory.
- Failure Impact: Students with less than 75% attendance are not allowed to sit for final exams and may receive an 'F' grade or forced withdrawal.
- Medical Leave: Only considered with proper documentation from recognized hospitals.

GRADING SYSTEM:
- Scale: 4.0 GPA Scale.
- Passing Marks: Generally 50% for undergraduates.
- Grade Distribution:
  - A Grade: 88% - 100% (4.0 GPA)
  - B Grade: 72% - 79% (3.0 GPA)
  - C Grade: 60% - 67% (2.0 GPA)

EXAMINATION RULES:
- Midterms: Conducted in the 8th week of the semester.
- Finals: Conducted in the 16th week of the semester.
- Mandatory Items: Valid Student ID and Exam Admit Card.
- Prohibited Items: Mobile phones, smartwatches, and programmable calculators are strictly banned in examination halls.

LIBRARY & RESOURCES:
- Timings: 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekdays.
- Digital Library: Access to HEC Digital Library is available 24/7 via the university network/portal.
- Book Issuance: Students can borrow a specific number of books using their IU ID card.

WI-FI & PORTAL ACCESS:
- Wi-Fi SSID: 'IU-Student'.
- Credentials: Login with Registration ID (Username) and Portal Password.
- Student Portal: Access at portal.iqra.edu.pk for result tracking, fee challans, and class schedules.
