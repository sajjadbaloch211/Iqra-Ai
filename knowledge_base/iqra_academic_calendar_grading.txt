### IQRA UNIVERSITY ACADEMIC POLICIES AND CALENDAR 2025-2026

ACADEMIC CALENDAR (FALL 2025 & SPRING 2026):
- Fall 2025 Classes Start: Monday, October 6, 2025.
- Fall 2025 Midterm Exams: November 24, 2025 - December 1, 2025.
- Fall 2025 Final Exams: January 25, 2026 - February 2, 2026.
- Result Announcement: February 9, 2026.
- Spring 2026 Admissions: Currently OPEN.

NEW GRADING SCHEME (EFFECTIVE SPRING 2025):
- Pass Threshold: 50 Marks (Minimum for passing a course).
- Undergraduate Requirement: Minimum CGPA 2.0 is mandatory for graduation.
- Grade Categories: 10 letter grades for Undergraduates, 7 for Graduates.
- Extended Grades: New grades like 'QQ', 'NQ', and 'XF' introduced for specific performance evaluation.
- Standardization: Uniform marks distribution and standardized exam durations applied across ALL campuses.

COURSE REGISTRATION POLICIES:
- Credit Hour Definition: 1 Theory Credit = 50 mins/class (16 contact hours/semester). 1 Lab Credit = 32 contact hours/semester.
- Registration Changes: Policy exists for adding/dropping or changing courses after initial registration, subject to specific deadlines and fees.
- Degree Duration: Specific minimum and maximum time limits exist for completing degrees (Associate, BS, MS, PhD).

CREDIT TRANSFER POLICY:
- Inter-Campus: Credits can be transferred between Iqra University campuses.
- External Transfer: Credits from other recognized institutions are accepted based on course equivalency, subject to HEC guidelines and university discretion.
