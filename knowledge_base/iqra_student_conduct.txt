### IQRA UNIVERSITY STUDENT CONDUCT AND ADMINISTRATIVE POLICIES

DRESS CODE POLICY:
- Requirement: Clean, smart casual attire that is socially and culturally acceptable.
- Prohibited: Shabby, dirty, unironed, or immodest/revealing clothing is strictly banned.
- ID Display: Students must display their University ID cards prominently at all times on campus.

MOBILE PHONE & TECHNOLOGY POLICY:
- In Class: Phones must be switched OFF (not just on silent). Violations may lead to confiscation until the end of the semester.
- In Exams: Mobile phones and smart gadgets are strictly prohibited in examination halls.

SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY:
- Official Content: Students cannot create pages resembling official IU pages.
- Ethical Conduct: Posting defamatory content about faculty, staff, students, or the university is strictly prohibited.
- REPRESENTATION: Students cannot join religious or political groups as university representatives without official approval.

GENERAL DISCIPLINE:
- Smoking/Drugs: Strictly prohibited on campus. Smoking is banned within 25 feet of any entrance/exit. Alcohol/drugs lead to immediate disciplinary action and reporting to authorities.
- Ragging/Bullying: Zero tolerance for ragging or harassment. Legally enforceable actions are taken against violators.
- Disciplinary Committee: Empowered to impose fines, warnings, suspension, or expulsion.

GENDER HARASSMENT & GRIEVANCE:
- Harassment Policy: Strict compliance with HEC and national laws to provide a safe, non-discriminatory environment.
- Complaints: Formal procedure available for academic, administrative, or interpersonal grievances. Non-retaliation protection for good-faith complainants.

SUPPORT FOR DISABILITY:
- Policy: Ensuring equal learning opportunities for differently-abled students in line with HEC guidelines.
