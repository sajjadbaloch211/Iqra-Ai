FACULTIES & DESIGNATIONS:
The university employs international researchers and industry experts as Professors, Associate Professors, Assistant Professors, and Lecturers. Many faculty members are ranked among the world's top 2% scientists.

MAJOR FACULTIES:
1. Faculty of Engineering, Science & Technology (FEST).
2. Faculty of Business Administration (FBA).
3. Faculty of Health Sciences & Pharmacy.
4. Faculty of Art, Design, Education, and Social Sciences (FADES).

RESEARCH & INNOVATION CENTERS:
- ORIC (Office of Research, Innovation & Commercialization): Established to foster research, industry collaboration, and commercialization.
- Research Clusters:
  - Smart Infrastructure & Sustainable Development (IoT, AR, Smart Farming).
  - Health & Wellness (Anxiety research, Machine Learning in Healthcare).
  - Education & Social Equity (Teaching research, Sign language communication).

SPECIALIZED LABORATORIES:
- Pharmacy Labs: Pharmacognosy, Microbiology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, BMS, Pharmaceutics, Industrial, and QC Labs (equipped with UV Spectrophotometers, Dissolution apparatus, etc.).
- Technology Labs: Advanced IT Labs, IoT Testbeds, and AI Labs.
