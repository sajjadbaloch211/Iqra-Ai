### IQRA UNIVERSITY ADMISSIONS 2026 - PROCEDURES AND DEADLINES

IMPORTANT DATES (SPRING 2026 INTAKE):
- Admissions: OPEN NOW for Spring 2026.
- Application Deadline (General): September 29, 2025.
- Application Deadline (Diploma/BS H-9 Campus): September 10, 2025.
- Classes Start (Prompt Engineering): August 27, 2025.

ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Undergraduate (General): Minimum 45% in HSSC (Intermediate) or equivalent.
- Pre-Medical Programs: Minimum 60% in HSSC (Pre-Medical).
- Engineering Programs: Minimum 60% in HSSC (Pre-Engineering). Entry test pass marks: 33%.
- MS/PhD Programs: Minimum CGPA 3.0/4.0 and 60% in GAT Subject or GRE.
- A-Levels/Foreign Students: Equivalence certificate from IBCC is mandatory.

ADMISSION PROCESS:
1. Online Application: Register at admissions.iqra.edu.pk.
2. Submit Details: Fill personal and academic history.
3. Fee Payment: Download and pay application fee challan.
4. Entrance Test: Computerized MCQ-based test on campus.
5. Interview: Some programs require a follow-up interview.
6. Merit List: Final selection based on test results and previous marks.

PROVISIONAL ADMISSION:
Candidates awaiting results (Result Awaiting) can apply provisionally. They must submit final results within 15 days of semester start. Failure to meet percentage criteria leads to immediate cancellation of admission. (Not applicable for MS/PhD).
