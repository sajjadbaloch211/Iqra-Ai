### IQRA UNIVERSITY CAMPUSES AND ACADEMIC DEPARTMENTS (2025-2026)

KARACHI CAMPUSES:
- Main Campus: Defence View, Shaheed-e-Millat Road Extension. (Headquarters)
- North Campus: Sector 7-B/1, North Karachi (Opposite Muhammad Shah Graveyard).
- Gulshan Campus 2 & Extension: Block 7, Gulshan-e-Iqbal.
- Bahria Town Campus: Located inside Bahria Town Karachi.
- Airport/Malir Campus: Near Malir Halt/Airport area.
- M9 Campus: Sector 8B, Scheme 33 (Near Super Highway).

OTHER CAMPUSES:
- Islamabad Campus (Sector H-9): Main Islamabad operations.
- Islamabad Campus (Chak Shahzad): Focusing on Allied Health and Social Sciences.
- Hyderabad Campus: Located in Hyderabad city.

ACADEMIC DEPARTMENTS & SPECIALIZATIONS:

1. COMPUTING & TECHNOLOGY:
   - BS Computer Science (BSCS)
   - BS Software Engineering
   - Master of Science in Cyber Security
   - Specialization: Prompt Engineering (Starting August 2025 at Main Campus)
   - Associate Degrees: Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Data Science.

2. BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION:
   - BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
   - BS Accounting & Finance
   - BS Entrepreneurship
   - BS Economics & Finance
   - MBA and PhD in Business Administration
   - Associate Degrees: Digital Marketing, Islamic Banking, Tourism & Hospitality.

3. ALLIED HEALTH SCIENCES:
   - Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm-D)
   - Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT)
   - BS Nursing, Medical Lab Technology (MLT), Radiology.
   - BS Human Nutrition & Dietetics.

4. MEDIA SCIENCES & ARTS:
   - BS Media Studies
   - Associate Degree in Animation & Film.

5. LAW:
   - Bachelor of Laws (LLB) - 5-year program.

6. FASHION & DESIGN:
   - Undergraduate programs in Fashion Design and Interior Design (Primarily Islamabad/Main Campus).
