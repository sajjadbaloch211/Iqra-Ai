3. ADMISSION CRITERIA (2025)
- General Requirements:
    - All applicants must clear the Iqra University Admission Test (IUAT) or provide valid NTS/HEC test scores.
    - Merit Calculation: 60% Academic Record (Intermediate/A-Levels) + 40% Entrance Test Score.
- Undergraduate Programs (BS/BBA):
    - Academic: Min 50% marks in Intermediate (HSSC) / Equivalent.
    - A-Levels: Equivalence Certificate from IBCC required (Min C grades usually).
    - BS Computer Science: Min 50% marks in Pre-Engineering or CS group.
- Graduate Programs (MBA/MS):
    - Academic: 16 years of education with Min 2.5 CGPA or 2nd Division.
    - Test: GAT (General) with 50% score or IU Internal Test.
    - MBA (2 Years): Requires 16 years of non-business education.
    - MBA (1.5 Years): Requires BBA (Honors) or equivalent business degree.
- PhD Programs:
    - Academic: 18 years of education (MS/MPhil) with Min 3.0/4.0 CGPA.
    - Test: GAT (Subject) or GRE required.
- Documents Required:
    - CNIC/B-Form copy.
    - Official Transcripts (Matric, Inter, Bachelors).
    - 4 Passport size photographs.

6. GRADING & EXAMS SYSTEM
- GPA Scale (4.0):
    - A  (87-100%): 4.0
    - B+ (80-86%): 3.5
    - B  (72-79%): 3.0
    - C+ (66-71%): 2.5
    - C  (60-65%): 2.0
    - F  (Below 60%): Fail
- Policies:
    - Attendance: Strict 80% mandatory attendance to sit for finals.
    - Probation: GPA < 2.0. Two consecutive probations result in dropout/suspension.
    - "I" Grade (Incomplete): Must be cleared in the subsequent semester.
11. ACADEMIC RULES & REGULATIONS
- Semester Freeze: Allowed for max 1 year. Application required in first 7 weeks.
- Course Withdrawal (W Grade): Allowed until roughly 2 weeks before finals. Does not impact GPA.
- Repeat Policy: Courses with C- or lower can be repeated. Higher grade replaces the lower one in GPA calc.
- Plagiarism: Zero tolerance. Assignments checked via Turnitin.
